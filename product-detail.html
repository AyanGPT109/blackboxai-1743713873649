Detailed Description

Our matte lipstick is designed for those who love bold, long-lasting color without the drying effect of traditional matte formulas. The creamy texture glides on smoothly and sets to a comfortable matte finish that won't feather or bleed.

The innovative formula contains nourishing ingredients that help to condition lips while providing intense color payoff. The angled bullet allows for precise application, making it easy to achieve the perfect pout.

Full Ingredients List

Ricinus Communis (Castor) Seed Oil, Cera Alba (Beeswax), Euphorbia Cerifera (Candelilla) Wax, Copernicia Cerifera (Carnauba) Wax, Tocopheryl Acetate (Vitamin E), Persea Gratissima (Avocado) Oil, Mica, Titanium Dioxide (CI 77891), Iron Oxides (CI 77491, CI 77492, CI 77499), Red 7 Lake (CI 15850).
